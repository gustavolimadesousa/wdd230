Become a Member of Espinho Chamber of Commerce

Membership Benefits

Non Profit

Cost: No fees.

Benefits:
  • Regular updates and newsletters to keep members informed about the organization's activities, achievements, and upcoming events.
  • Opportunities to connect with like-minded individuals or organizations working towards a common goal.

Bronze Membership

Cost: This is the basic level of membership with the lowest cost.

Benefits:
  • Access to the organization's newsletter and updates.
  • Invitation to annual general meetings.
  • Opportunity to volunteer at events.
  • Basic member-only discounts for organization events and merchandise.
  • Recognition as a Bronze member on the organization's website and materials.

Silver Membership

Cost: A moderate increase in membership fees.

Benefits:
  • All benefits of Bronze membership.
  • Access to exclusive webinars, workshops, or training sessions.
  • Priority registration and discounts for special events and conferences.
  • Silver member badge or certificate.
  • Inclusion in the organization's annual report as a Silver member.

Gold Membership

Cost: The highest level of membership with the highest fees.

Benefits:
  • All benefits of Bronze and Silver memberships.
  • Access to members-only networking events and exclusive seminars.
  • Premium advertising space in the organization's publications or website.
  • Significant discounts on all organization events and merchandise.
  • Gold member plaque or certificate.
  • Personalized recognition and appreciation during the annual general meeting.
