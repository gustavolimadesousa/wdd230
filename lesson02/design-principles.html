White Space and Clean Design

Apple

apple.com

Apple set the bar for including breathing room into their design as one of the pioneers of employing whitespace on the web to highlight a product.

In this case, Apple employs both active and passive whitespace to lead the user through the information without any problems. Active whitespace is used across the website to bring the user's attention to certain parts down the page.

Alignment

Nike

nike.com

Every element on Nike’s website has a visual connection with another element whether that’s an obvious connection (such as in a table) or a subtle one (invisible lines). The items are not randomly placed. The visual connection creates a pleasant-looking document. It is noticeable the use of a grid and strong alignments.

Contrast

Uber

uber.com

When it comes to contrast Uber has always shown an excellent example. The company’s website color scheme consists of only black and white. Using only two colors keeps the website minimalistic and neat. There’s no better contrast than black and white.

The consistency between the color palettes across the platforms has given Uber a true identity with a contrast that shows modernity and innovation.
